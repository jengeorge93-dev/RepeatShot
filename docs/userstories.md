# User Stories

## Homeowner

I'm remodeling my kitchen.

I want every progress photo to look identical.

So I can create an amazing before & after.

---

## Gardener

I want to photograph my tomatoes every week.

I don't want to guess where I stood last time.

---

## Dog Owner

I want monthly pictures of my puppy.

I want the same framing every month.

---

## Parent

I want yearly birthday photos in the same location.

---

## Contractor

I need consistent progress photos for clients.

---

## Realtor

I want listing transformations to look professional.

---

## Fitness User

I want body progress photos that actually line up.

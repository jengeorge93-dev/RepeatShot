# RepeatShot

## Vision

RepeatShot helps people recreate the exact same photo over time.

Instead of trying to remember where they stood or how they framed the shot, users simply open the project, line up a ghost image, and capture the next photo.

The result is professional-looking before & after comparisons and time-lapse progress photos.

---

## Mission

Make documenting progress effortless.

---

## The Problem

People document progress all the time:

- Home renovations
- Gardens
- Fitness
- Weight loss
- Puppies growing
- Babies growing
- Construction projects
- DIY builds
- Seasonal changes
- Landscapes

But every photo is taken from a different angle.

This makes comparisons less satisfying.

---

## Our Solution

Remember the original shot.

Guide the user back to that position.

Create beautiful progress timelines.

---

## Core Principles

Simple.

Fast.

No account required.

No social feed.

No unnecessary editing tools.

One job:
Help users recreate the same photo.

## Long-Term Vision

Become the easiest way to document progress over time.
